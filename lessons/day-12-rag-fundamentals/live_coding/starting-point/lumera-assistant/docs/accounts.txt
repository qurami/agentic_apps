Banca Lumera — Current and Savings Accounts

The Lumera current account has a maintenance fee of 2.50 euros per month.
The fee is waived in any month where the average account balance stays
above 5,000 euros. There is no fee to open or to close a current account.

The Lumera savings account pays 2.10% gross interest per year, credited
every quarter. There is no maintenance fee on savings accounts, but the
account must be linked to a Lumera current account.

Monthly account statements are available free of charge in the app and
the web banking portal. Paper statements sent by post cost 1.50 euros
per statement.

Accounts can be opened online with a valid identity document and an
Italian tax code (codice fiscale). Joint accounts require both holders
to complete identification.
