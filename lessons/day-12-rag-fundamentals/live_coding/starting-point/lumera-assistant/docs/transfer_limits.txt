Banca Lumera — Transfer Limits and Timing

Online transfers from a Lumera current account are limited to 5,000
euros per single transfer. Transfers above this limit must be ordered
at a branch, where they are subject to additional verification.

There is no fixed limit on the number of transfers per day, but the
total daily amount ordered online cannot exceed 10,000 euros.

Standard SEPA credit transfers are settled within one business day.
Transfers ordered after 17:30, or on weekends and holidays, are
processed on the next business day.

SEPA instant transfers arrive within about 10 seconds, at any hour and
on any day, and cost 1 euro per transfer. Instant transfers share the
same 5,000 euro per-transfer limit.

Transfers between two Lumera accounts are always free and immediate.
