Banca Lumera — Branches and Opening Hours

Banca Lumera branches are open Monday to Friday, from 8:30 to 13:00 and
from 14:30 to 16:30. All branches are closed on weekends and on Italian
national holidays.

The main branches are in Napoli (Via Toledo 145), Roma (Via del Corso
88) and Milano (Corso Buenos Aires 21). Smaller branches operate in
most provincial capitals.

Cash operations at the counter are available until 13:00. Appointments
with an advisor can be booked through the assistant or the app and are
also available outside counter hours, until 16:30.

The full and up-to-date branch list, with addresses and any local
schedule changes, is available on the Banca Lumera website.
