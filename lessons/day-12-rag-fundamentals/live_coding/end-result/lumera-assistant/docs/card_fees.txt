Banca Lumera — Payment Card Fees

The Lumera debit card has no yearly fee: it comes free with every
current account.

The Lumera credit card costs 45 euros per year. The yearly fee is waived
if the total card spending in the year exceeds 6,000 euros.

Replacing a lost, stolen or damaged card costs 10 euros. The replacement
card is delivered within 5 working days; blocking the old card is free
and immediate.

Cash withdrawals at Lumera ATMs are free. Withdrawals at other banks'
ATMs in the euro area cost 2 euros each. Withdrawals outside the euro
area cost 2% of the amount, with a minimum of 2.50 euros, plus the
currency conversion applied at the interbank rate.

Contactless payments are enabled by default up to 50 euros without PIN.
